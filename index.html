Rhino Species and Habitats

Black Rhino

The black rhinoceros (Diceros bicornis) is native to Eastern and Southern Africa. They are known for their hooked upper lip and are primarily browsers, feeding on trees and bushes.

White Rhino

The white rhinoceros (Ceratotherium simum) is the largest rhino species and is divided into two subspecies: the Southern white rhino and the critically endangered Northern white rhino.
